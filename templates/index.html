Forensic Capabilities

Built on OpenSource Computer Vision (OpenCV), our engine ignores noise and focuses on the geometric truth of biometric data.

CLAHE Enhancement

Contrast Limited Adaptive Histogram Equalization normalizes ridge pressure, making faint prints readable even from poor sensors.

SIFT Feature Extraction

Scale-Invariant Feature Transform identifies unique ridge bifurcations and endings, creating a digital signature resistant to rotation.

RANSAC Validation

Random Sample Consensus geometrically verifies matches, eliminating false positives caused by dust, sensor noise, or partial prints.
